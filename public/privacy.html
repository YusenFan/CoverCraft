Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

Introduction

CoverCraft AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web application and Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Resume Content: When you upload a resume (PDF, DOCX, or DOC), we extract the text content to generate your cover letter. This content is processed temporarily and is not stored on our servers.
  • Job Posting Information: Text content from job posting pages you're viewing (when using the Chrome extension) or job URLs you provide (when using the web app).

Information Collected Automatically

  • Language Preference: Your preferred output language is stored locally in your browser to remember your settings.
  • Usage Analytics: We use Vercel Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics (page views, general geographic region). This data cannot identify individual users.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Generate personalized cover letters based on your resume and target job
  • Remember your language preferences for future visits
  • Improve our service through anonymous analytics

We do NOT:

  • Store your resume content on our servers
  • Store generated cover letters
  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Share your personal information with anyone except as necessary to provide the service

Third-Party Services

OpenAI

We use OpenAI's GPT-4 model to generate cover letters. When you request a cover letter, your resume text and job posting content are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI's use of this data is governed by their Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

Vercel

Our application is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server logs. See Vercel's Privacy Policy for details.

Chrome Extension Specific

The CoverCraft AI Chrome extension requests the following permissions:

activeTab

Allows reading the current page content only when you click the extension icon. Never activates automatically.

scripting

Enables text extraction from job posting pages. Does not modify any page content.

storage

Stores your language preference locally on your device. No personal data is stored.

host_permissions

Required to read job postings from various job boards and make API requests to our server.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • API keys are stored securely on our server, never exposed to clients
  • Resume and job content is processed in memory and not persisted to disk
  • Local storage data remains on your device and is never transmitted

Data Retention

We do not retain your personal data. Resume content and job posting text are processed in real-time to generate your cover letter and are immediately discarded after the response is sent. We do not maintain any database of user-submitted content.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your locally stored preferences at any time through your browser settings
  • Uninstall the Chrome extension to remove all extension-related data
  • Request information about what data we process (answer: none is stored)
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

CoverCraft AI is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of CoverCraft AI after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
